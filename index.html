Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below, each with their own value. Click on the crystals to add to your total score. You win the game by mathcing your total score to the random number, you lose if your total goes above random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of the crystals. Begin!
